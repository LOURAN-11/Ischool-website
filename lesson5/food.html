Famous Egyptian Foods

Koshary

Koshary is Egypt's national dish. It's a mix of pasta, lentils, rice, chickpeas, fried onions, and spicy tomato sauce. Delicious and filling!

Basbousa

Basbousa is a sweet semolina cake soaked in syrup. It's soft, sweet, and topped with almonds or coconut. A popular Egyptian dessert!
